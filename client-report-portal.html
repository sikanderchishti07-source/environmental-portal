How to access your report

  • 1
    Locate the security key sent to your registered mobile number via SMS from AECON.
  • 2
    Type or paste the key exactly as received — including any hyphens — into the field on the left.
  • 3
    Click Access My Reports. Your report will appear instantly below the form.
  • 4
    Use the Download PDF button to save a local copy of your report.
